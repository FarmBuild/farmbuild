Hack Day Invitation

Register for FarmBuild I am interested in FarmBuild

An opportunity to get down and dirty with the FarmBuild code and have a bit of fun! Working in an agile fashion, the day will build user-stories from FarmBuild's Github repositories.

WHEN: Wednesday 27th July 2016

TIME: 9am to 5pm

LOCATION: Spatial Vision, Level 4, 575 Bourke St, Melbourne

Before the Hack Day, please:

  • check out the FarmBuild webpages
  • watch the FarmBuild webinar on YouTube
  • make yourself familiar with the FarmBuild GitHub repositories

Please note we have to limit the number of attendees for this session to ensure an effective day for those involved.

We recommend that you have some working knowledge of JavaScript to participate in the Hack Day.

Working in small groups, the day will encourage participates to work through an example of their own creation.

Please share this invitation with colleagues to form your own small group, or come along to join in a group on the day.

You will need to BYO device for the day, but WiFi will be provided.

Catering provided.Please RSVP to Gemma Heemskerk by Friday 22nd July with any dietary requirements.
